Mi Goreng Instant Noodles Recipe

Photo by Gundula Vogel
  • Back to Home

Ingredients

  • Mi Goreng Noodles
  • 2 Eggs
  • Water
  • Sprig of green onion
  • Seasoning packet

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add eggs for 7 minutes to boiling water
  3. Take eggs out and submerge in cold water
  4. Peel eggs
  5. Add noodles to boiling water for 2 minutes
  6. Drain noodle water
  7. Add seasoning packet
  8. Chop up green onion and add to top of noodles
  9. Slice boiled eggs in half
  10. Add eggs and eat!
